Training & Teaching

We build capacity through practical courses, field schools, and tailored teaching in groundwater science and environmental technology.

Programs & Workshops

Managed Aquifer Recharge (MAR) Training

A hands-on workshop combining geophysical site assessment, recharge design, and long-term monitoring strategies using low-cost sensors and open models.

TEM & Hydrogeophysics

Field-based short course on transient electromagnetic methods for groundwater investigations, including inversion, visualization, and interpretation of tTEM data.

Edcrop Training Module

Interactive training in water balance and ET modeling using our Edcrop tool, ideal for understanding wetlands, recharge, and field-scale hydrology.

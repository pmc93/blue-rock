Monitoring Injection Wells with ERT

Visualizing the subsurface behavior of injected water in real time using geophysical tools.

Use Case

Managed aquifer recharge and chemical remediation rely on successful delivery of fluids. ERT lets us track this in space and time.

Approach

  • Cross-borehole or surface-to-borehole ERT arrays
  • Repeated surveys during and after injection
  • Time-lapse inversion using pyGIMLi or ResIPy

Results are animated and interpreted to identify spreading, preferential flow, and stagnation zones.

Impact

Better injection design, improved safety margins, and long-term optimization of recharge and cleanup efforts.

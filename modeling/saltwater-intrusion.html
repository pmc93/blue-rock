Mapping Saltwater Intrusion with ERT

Identifying salinity front movement in coastal aquifers through geophysical imaging.

Why It Matters

Saltwater intrusion threatens freshwater supplies in coastal zones. ERT allows us to visualize the salinity wedge and assess aquifer vulnerability over time.

How We Do It

  • 2D/3D ERT lines perpendicular to coast
  • High-density Wenner-Schlumberger arrays
  • Inversion with RES2DINV / pyGIMLi
  • Calibrated with borehole salinity profiles

Result

Clients receive maps of intrusion depth and extent, and time-lapse options show seasonal or pumping-related movement.
